Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need a microphone?

Nope, this is not an electronic tuner. This tuner serves as a reference for the notes you need when you tune your guitar, but it's up to your ears to do the actual tuning work. This is what it means to tune your guitar by ear and it's a lot more fun to do. Try it out.

Can I use my keyboard to navigate?

Absolutely! Number keys 1 through 6 correspond to their strings.

You can also advance to adjacent strings using the left and right arrow keys.

Pressing ESC will stop all the notes ringing.
